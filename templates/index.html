What We Are

Bahari is a modern interpretation of a traditional Irish pub. Our name means "ocean" in Irish, and we aim to bring the spirit of the sea to our landlocked location. Our space is designed with relaxed elegance, and our menu features fresh takes on classic Irish and British cuisine.

Bahari was founded in 2023 by a group of friends who wanted to create a place where people could gather and enjoy good food and drinks. We opened our first location in the heart of downtown, and it quickly became a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. Over the years, we've expanded to other cities, but our commitment to quality and community has remained the same.
