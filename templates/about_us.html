About Bahari Irish Pub

Bahari is a modern interpretation of a traditional Irish pub. Our name means "ocean" in Irish, and we aim to bring the spirit of the sea to our landlocked location. Our space is designed with relaxed elegance, and our menu features fresh takes on classic Irish and British cuisine.

View Gallery

Our History

Bahari was founded in 2002 by a group of friends who wanted to create a place where people could gather and enjoy good food and drinks. We opened our first location in the heart of downtown, and it quickly became a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. Over the years, we've expanded to other cities, but our commitment to quality and community has remained the same.

Our Philosophy

At Bahari, we believe in the power of great hospitality. We strive to create an environment that is warm and welcoming, where everyone feels like they belong. We're committed to using the best ingredients, preparing them with care, and serving them with a smile. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch or a leisurely dinner, we want you to have a wonderful experience.
